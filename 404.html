About the Project

We are pleased to present our unique gaming server complex Lineage2 Gracia Final & Interlude — without donations and with maximum comfort for players!

Easy Registration

Our servers feature auto-registration. All you need to do is download our client and launch it. In the game, simply enter your username and password. Please note: do not use the username and password that you use on other servers to ensure the security of your account.

Classic Builds

Our servers offer classic builds that are as close as possible to the official versions of the game. You will be able to enjoy the game in its true form, without unnecessary modifications.

Support and Community

All additional information about the game is available directly in the client or in our community. Here you can ask any questions and receive help from other players.

Join Us!

We look forward to seeing you in our gaming world! Join us and become part of an exciting adventure!
